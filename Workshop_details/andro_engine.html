About Workshop

One Of The Embryonic Innovations In Automobile With The Help Of Artificial Intelligence. Forward In The Way To Mobile Controlled Vehicle We Have Developed Andro- Engine. Andro- Engine Is A Combination Of Android And Engine With Interface Of A Electronics Device Arduino . It Is A Project Based Workshop/training Where We Are Going To Control Our IC Engine With Help Of Android App.

All Study Material Would Be Provided
Workshop Will Be Of 16 Hours (2 Days, 8 Hrs Each)
